Backcountry Adventures

Seven years ago I suddenly became sick and found myself in the hospital for over a week. When I was finally able to leave, the doctor told me that I needed to change my lifestyle and become more active or I would find myself back in the hospital for good. That day I committed to changing my lifestyle and began looking for ways to do so that I could actually enjoy.

As I began experimenting with the outdoors, I was surprised to find a hidden love for them. My adventures started small but quickly became more elaborate until I could hardly stand to be away from nature in one way or another. I hope adventurs and lessons I share can inspire you to find or further develop your own passions!
